ABOUT US

As the saying goes, “To plant garden is to believe in tomorrow”, we always dreamed of sharing this beautiful journey of growing and nurturing bonsai plants ensuring the sound mental health of an individual. It all started when we saw the attractive bonsai plants in the Japanese cartoons specifically ‘NINJA HATTORI “in early ages. As we were eager to know about shaping beautiful shrub, we researched everything about it. We successfully established our company THE BONSAI PLANT on Jan 1,2023 based in Kathmandu, Nepal and today we are shipping the grown bonsai plants at your doorstep along with the tool kit and manuals for the beginners. Welcome to the brotherhood of bonsai friends! The common goal of founders of THE BONSAI SHOP was to make you know about the philosophy of life by growing bonsai plants. We Team 6 don’t want any individual loose a chance to become creative, compassionate, and consistent as we chose to be one in very short span of time. It was hard but not impossible to gather skilled members who coordinated to bring this beautiful ideas and output to you all. From having no idea about how the websites were built to building this amazing e-commerce website, The Bonsai plant having all the features was hard yet valuable journey to all the skilled folks .
